How to join Boy Scouts

  1. Request to join the t485 google groups to start receiving email updates.
  2. Complete Attachment No.1 through No.7, print out, sign and date and return to Membership Chair by the next Troop meeting (check our calendar).
  3. Complete the original Boy Scouts of America Youth Application form which you will receive at the committee meeting, sign and date and return to Membership Chair as soon as possible.
  4. Bring a check of $100 (for this year) payable to T485 Membership Chair. If joined mid-year the rate is prorated ($12.5/month for this year, count from the month you received the registration form to December of the same year).
  5. Purchase scout uniform and scout handbook (New version). See detailed information on Attachment No. 8. Contact Chienling ( Lo_chien@yahoo.com) for Class B Shirt and neckerchief, and contact Margaret ( margart_zhao@hotmail.com ) for Unit number "485" patch.
  6. Complete online training. See details on Attachment No. 1
  7. Review T485 program policy, sub-committee description and parent handbook. (Attachment No. 9, No.10 and No. 11).
  8. Attend New Parent Orientation. The date and time of the next New Parent Orientation session will be emailed to you.

Uniform Guide

Scout Oath

On my honor I will do my best to do my duty to God and my country and to obey the Scout Law; to help other people at all times; to keep myself physically strong, mentally awake, and morally straight.

Scout Law

A scout is: Trustworthy, Loyal, Helpful, Friendly, Courteous, Kind, Obedient, Cheerful, Thrifty, Brave, Clean, and Reverent.
